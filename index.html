How to Play:

  • Click the tiles to uncover a square. (But if you click on a mine, you lose the game!)
  • Right-click to place a flag where you think a mine will be found.
  • If a tile contains a number, that tells you how many mines are located in the neighboring tiles.
  • To win, uncover all the tiles witout clicking on a mine.
